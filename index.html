Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 9, 2026

Holdout ("the App") is a mobile game developed by an independent developer ("we", "us", "our"). This policy explains what data is collected when you use the App and how it is handled.

1. Data We Collect Directly

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. The App does not require an account to play.

Game data (your best time, settings, purchases, and preferences) is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. This data is not transmitted to our servers.

2. Data Collected by Third Parties

The App uses third-party services that may collect data as described below:

  • Google AdMob — serves advertisements within the App. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, ad interaction data, and general location information to deliver personalized or non-personalized ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — processes in-app purchases. RevenueCat receives an anonymous user identifier and transaction data necessary to validate purchases. No personal information is shared. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

3. Advertising & Ad Personalization

AdMob may use device information to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking

4. In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed through Google Play (Android) or the App Store (iOS). We do not receive or store your payment information. Purchase records are managed by RevenueCat to enable purchase restoration across devices.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided data through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information

We do not sell your personal information. AdMob's data collection for ad personalization may constitute "sharing" under CCPA. You can opt out via your device's ad personalization settings described above.

7. Data Retention

Game data is stored locally on your device and persists until you uninstall the App or clear its data. We do not retain any data on our servers. Third-party data retention is governed by their respective privacy policies.

8. Security

Local game data is stored using standard platform storage mechanisms. We do not transmit personal data, so there is no network-level data to secure on our end. Third-party services use their own security measures.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: holdoutgame@gmail.com
